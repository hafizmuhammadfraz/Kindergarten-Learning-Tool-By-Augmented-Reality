About The App

About

It is a kindergarden learning app by Augmented Reality (AR) through which your children can learn ENGLISH, URDU, MATH, ARTS by tracing and can view regarding models in Augmented Reality. It also has a Animals portion where kids can learn about animals and can view them in Augmented Reality.

How to view models in Augmented Reality?

Select AR portion from any Subject from English,Urdu,Math.
Point the camera towards Book given in hardcopy or softcopy models will be shown.
Similarly in Zoo portion point the camera towards ground animal AR model will be shown of selected Animal.

How to do Tracing?

Select any portion containing tracing part.
Letters/ Aplhabets will be shown to you just select them and trace them according to the given points after tracing those points completely your letter / Alphabet will be traced.
